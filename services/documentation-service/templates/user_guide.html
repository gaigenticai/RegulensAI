AML/KYC Module User Guide

1

Customer Onboarding

Complete workflow for onboarding new customers with KYC verification.

Individual Customer Fields
first_name
Required

Customer's legal first name as it appears on government-issued ID. Maximum 100 characters.

last_name
Required

Customer's legal last name as it appears on government-issued ID. Maximum 100 characters.

date_of_birth
Date (YYYY-MM-DD)

Customer's date of birth. Used for age verification and risk assessment. Must be 18+ for most services.

nationality
ISO 3166-1 Alpha-3

Customer's nationality using 3-letter country codes (e.g., USA, CAN, GBR). Used for sanctions screening and risk assessment.

Important: All customer information must match government-issued identification documents exactly.
2

Risk Assessment

Automated and manual risk assessment process.

risk_rating
Enum

Calculated risk level: LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, VERY_HIGH. Automatically determined based on multiple factors.

  • LOW: Standard processing, minimal documentation
  • MEDIUM: Additional verification may be required
  • HIGH: Enhanced due diligence required
  • VERY_HIGH: Senior approval and extensive documentation required

Troubleshooting

Common Login Problems:
  • Invalid credentials: Verify username and password are correct
  • Account locked: Contact administrator after 5 failed attempts
  • Session expired: Log out completely and log back in
  • Browser issues: Clear cache and cookies, try incognito mode

Performance Optimization:
  • Slow loading: Check internet connection, try refreshing page
  • Browser compatibility: Use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
  • Large datasets: Use filters to reduce data volume
  • Memory issues: Close unused browser tabs
